ABOUT ME

Hey. I'm Dinh Cuong. I'm Vietnamese.

I note the moments of my journey into the adult world with my Canon.

I'm not a photographer, I'm a normal person who knows how to take photos. I really love photography. I want to create great photos, capture memorable moments. So I carry my Canon everywhere. If you really want to remember a moment, hold the camera and take a photo. The moment never waits for you to think, catch it whenever you can.
